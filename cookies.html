Julia's Favorite Cookie Recipes

  • Ghirardelli Chocolate Chip Cookies

    • One of my favorite recipies is these Chocolate Chip Cookies from Ghirardelli. I have been making these for as long as I can remember, and I love them so much. This cookie has a light buttery texture on the inside, but a light crispy layer on the outside. This paired with the chocolate chips is one of the best things you will ever taste.
